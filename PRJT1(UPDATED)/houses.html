At Hogwarts, students are sorted into one of four houses, each representing unique values and traits that define their journey.

The Four Houses

Gryffindor

Founded by Godric Gryffindor, this house values bravery, courage, and chivalry. Notable members include Harry Potter and Albus Dumbledore.

Slytherin

Founded by Salazar Slytherin, this house values ambition, cunning, and leadership. Famous Slytherins include Severus Snape and Draco Malfoy.

Ravenclaw

Founded by Rowena Ravenclaw, this house values intelligence, wisdom, and creativity. Well-known members include Luna Lovegood and Cho Chang.

Hufflepuff

Founded by Helga Hufflepuff, this house values loyalty, patience, and hard work. Notable Hufflepuffs include Cedric Diggory and Newt Scamander.
