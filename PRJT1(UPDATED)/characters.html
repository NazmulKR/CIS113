Discover the legendary characters of the Harry Potter series, each possessing unique magical abilities and playing a crucial role in the wizarding world.

Key Characters

Harry Potter

The Boy Who Lived, Harry is the protagonist of the series, orphaned as a baby and later becoming the wizard who defeats Voldemort.

Hermione Granger

Brilliant and resourceful, Hermione excels in magic and is instrumental in helping Harry overcome numerous challenges.

Ron Weasley

Harry's loyal best friend, Ron comes from a loving wizarding family and proves his bravery throughout the series.

Albus Dumbledore

The wise and powerful headmaster of Hogwarts, Dumbledore plays a key role in guiding Harry against Voldemort.

Severus Snape

Though appearing antagonistic, Snape's true loyalty is revealed as he secretly protects Harry due to his love for Lily Potter.

Lord Voldemort

The Dark Lord, feared by all, who created Horcruxes to gain immortality but was ultimately defeated by Harry.
