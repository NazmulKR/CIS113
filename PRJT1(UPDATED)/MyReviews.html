My Personal Review

The Harry Potter series is more than just a collection of movies; it is a story that has captured the hearts of millions. Watching Harry's journey from a young boy discovering magic to the hero who defeats Voldemort is an unforgettable experience. The series is filled with brilliant storytelling, deep character development, and magical world-building that make it timeless. My personal favorite moment is when Snape's true loyalty is revealed, showing that his love for Lily Potter guided his every action.

Popular Fan Theories

  • Dumbledore is Dead: This theory suggests that Dumbledore represents Death from The Tale of the Three Brothers, as he gives Harry the Invisibility Cloak, possesses the Elder Wand, and guides him in the afterlife.
  • Neville as the Chosen One: The prophecy could have applied to either Harry or Neville, meaning that Neville could have been the one destined to defeat Voldemort.
  • Crookshanks' Secret Identity: Some fans believe that Hermione's cat, Crookshanks, was once the Potter family's pet, as he instantly recognizes Scabbers (Pettigrew) as an untrustworthy animagus.
