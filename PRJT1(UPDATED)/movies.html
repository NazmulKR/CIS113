Movie List

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone (2001)

Harry learns he is a wizard and attends Hogwarts, where he discovers the Sorcerer's Stone and battles Voldemort's influence.

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (2002)

Harry uncovers the legend of the Chamber of Secrets and defeats the Basilisk to save Hogwarts.

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (2004)

Harry learns the truth about Sirius Black and his connection to his parents' past.

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (2005)

Harry is forced into the Triwizard Tournament, where Voldemort's return is set into motion.

Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (2007)

Harry faces the Ministry's denial of Voldemort's return and forms Dumbledore's Army.

Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (2009)

Harry learns about Voldemort's Horcruxes while Snape's true allegiance is questioned.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1 (2010)

Harry, Ron, and Hermione search for Horcruxes while evading Death Eaters.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2 (2011)

The final battle at Hogwarts leads to Voldemort's defeat and the conclusion of Harry's journey.
