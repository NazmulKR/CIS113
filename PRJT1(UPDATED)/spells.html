Spells are the essence of the magical world, utilized for defense, utility, and battle. From protective enchantments to destructive curses, magic plays a vital role in wizardry.

Popular Spells

Expelliarmus

Known as the Disarming Charm, this spell is often used in duels to knock away an opponent's wand.

Expecto Patronum

A defensive spell that conjures a Patronus, protecting the caster from Dementors.

Unforgivable Curses

Dark spells including Avada Kedavra (the Killing Curse), Crucio (the Torture Curse), and Imperio (the Control Curse), all forbidden due to their harmful nature.

Lumos & Accio

Lumos creates light from a wand, while Accio summons objects from a distance.
